# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Two audiences. Primary: adults (and parents of children) seeking mental health support —
people at a crossroads, looking for meaning and direction, not just symptom relief.
Secondary: psychology professionals (psychologists, students) seeking clinical supervision,
study groups, and specialized training.
Context: both audiences approach with some emotional weight; the site must feel safe,
serious, and warmly human — never clinical or transactional.

## Product Purpose
Present LOGOS Psicologia Clínica and Dr. Saulo Magalhães as a trustworthy, intellectually
grounded practice rooted in Phenomenological-Existential psychology and Logotherapy.
Convert visitors into booked consultations or WhatsApp contacts. Differentiate from the
generic healthcare template aesthetic that defines all competitors.

## Brand Personality
Human, calm, intellectually grounded. A psychologist who is also a professor and researcher —
rigorous but warm. The practice cares about meaning (logos = word/reason/meaning), not just
wellness metrics.

## Anti-references
Hospitals and clinical whites. SaaS products with teal + icon grids.
Cold, sterile minimalism. Generic healthcare templates (Bootstrap + Font Awesome + soft teal).
Any design that could be mistaken for a dentist, a tech startup, or a wellness app.

## Design Principles
1. Meaning over wellness-speak — every word earns its place; no filler affirmations
2. Warmth through substance — trust is built by depth, not decorative softness
3. Break the healthcare mold — if it could be a dentist's site, it's wrong
4. The professional IS the brand — Dr. Saulo's voice, story, and approach are front and center
5. Calm confidence — nothing urgent, nothing loud; the design breathes

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Reduce motion support (prefers-reduced-motion). High contrast text.
Mental health context: avoid triggering imagery or overwhelming visual density.
